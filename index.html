Who are We?

Our Squadron is comprised of a wide range of people from various backgrounds; Active/Former Military, Airline Pilots, Train Conductors, Developers and many more. We welcome any and all who share the joy of MilSim games and are looking to have a great time! We strive to foster a positive environment and are able to offer an introduction to DCS with a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

Sound interesting? Check out our Contact page for more information.

How do I join?

We do have a few basic requirments for new recruits.

  1. Be 18+ Years of Age
  2. Have access to Discord and a Microphone
  3. Agree to our Rules of Engagement
  4. Be willing to learn and improve

We operate on the DCS Open Beta, so it is advised to ensure you are on the latest version.
